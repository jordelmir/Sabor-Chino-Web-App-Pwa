### Title: "Sabor Chino PWA: Comprehensive Technical Brand Identity Integration and Design System"

**Overall Vision and Style:** A high-fidelity, photorealistic UI design system showcase. The scene depicts a professional design workspace, focusing on a primary mobile device running the PWA, with supporting desktop mockups and detailed design specification panels. The overall aesthetic must maintain the premium, high-quality material feel of image_0.png, using rendered metal, enamel, and rich textures. All textual elements must be preserved verbatim.

---

**1. Technical Color Palette Specification (Verbatim and Hex):**

* **Primary Brand Red:** Use the specific, deep, textured red from the velvet background and enamel panels. Use #800000 (Crimson Red) and #A52A2A (Brown-Red) as base values, applied as a rich, textured fabric.
* **Primary Accent Gold/Brass:** The precise burnished brass/gold of the frames, dragons, text, and lanterns. Use #D4AF37 (Metallic Gold) and #B8860B (Dark Goldenrod) for metallic rendering.
* **Text Black Satin:** The burnished, non-reflective black of the central text panel and text background. Use #1A1A1A (Dark Charcoal).
* **Text Cream Enamel:** The clean, cream-colored enamel of the text "SABOR CHINO". Use #FDF5E6 (Old Lace).
* **Secondary Yellow:** The bright, saturated yellow of the lanterns. Use #FFD700 (Gold).

**2. Detailed PWA UI Implementation Breakdown:**

* **A. Splash Screen (Loading Screen):**
* **Action:** Displays the *entire* emblem logo from image_0.png as a central element.
* **Details:** The full circular emblem, with all three dragons, all three lanterns, the central banner, and all verbatim text, must be perfectly scaled and centered. The background must be the specific textured velvet red with the golden waves, creating a luxurious depth-of-field effect.
* **Verbatim Text Check:** Ensure "HERMANOS BALMACEDA", "SABOR CHINO", "COMIDA CHI", and "INA EXPRESS" are all present and legible.


* **B. App Icon and PWA Pinned Icon:**
* **Action:** Creation of scalable app icons (e.g., adaptive icons for Android, square for iOS).
* **Details:** A simplified circular icon that focuses on the central large dragon and the text 'SABOR CHINO', with the outer golden frame. The background is the deep red enamel.
* **Implementation:** Show mockups on mobile home screens (Android, iOS) with adaptive icons.


* **C. App Bar (Header Navigation):**
* **Action:** Redesigning the standard app header.
* **Details:** The background is the satin black (#1A1A1A). On the far left, a simplified version of the central dragon (the large one) is rendered in metallic gold (#D4AF37) and functions as the branding element. Text "SABOR CHINO" is centered in cream enamel (#FDF5E6). Icons (menu, cart, profile) are custom-designed, with the menu icon perhaps being a simplified gold dragon claw or scale.


* **D. Section Dividers and Motifs:**
* **Action:** Using specific graphic elements for UI structure.
* **Details:**
* **The Large Central Dragon:** Replicated exactly and used as a major section header divider (e.g., above the "Menu Categories" section).
* **The Small Bottom Dragon:** Replicated exactly and used as a subtle category separator (e.g., between "Appetizers" and "Main Courses").
* **The Golden Waves:** Use the velvet background's wave texture as a subtle, high-key background overlay on key call-to-action cards (e.g., for "Featured Dish" or "Special Offer").




* **E. Typography:**
* **Action:** Defining text styles.
* **Details:** The PWA should use a clean, modern, sans-serif font for body text to ensure maximum readability on mobile. However, major headings should adopt the specific, dimensional, cream-enameled font style of "SABOR CHINO" (a stylized, blocky, ornamental sans-serif) with the gold outline effect. Text must be rendered dimensionally and with reflections, not just as flat text.
